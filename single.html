Celebrating Onam With Our Cubbies

Onam is a super fun celebration for kids, filled with excitement and joy! We start by making beautiful pookalam, carefully arranging colorful flowers in intricate designs that brighten up our home and symbolize the spirit of the festival. Each day, we add more flowers, making it a fun activity to do with family and friends. Then comes the best part‚Äîhelping in the kitchen! We taste all the delicious dishes like sambar, avial, and sweet payasam that will be part of the grand Onam sadhya. The wonderful aromas fill the house, and we eagerly await the feast. Dressing up in our traditional clothes makes us feel festive and connected to our culture. We love listening to stories about King Mahabali, the beloved ruler who visits us during Onam, bringing happiness and prosperity. It‚Äôs so much fun to hear how he makes sure everyone is happy and cared for! As the celebrations continue, we gather with friends to play traditional games like tug-of-war and even make our own mini boat races! Dancing to cheerful music fills the air with laughter and excitement, and we all join in the fun, showcasing our best moves. Onam is truly all about joy, togetherness, and making unforgettable memories with family. From the colorful decorations to the delicious food and joyful games, it‚Äôs a time when we come together to celebrate our culture and the bonds we share. Happy Onam!

Celebrating Onam With Our Teachers

Onam is a vibrant celebration that brings teachers and staff together in a spirit of unity and joy! The day begins with the beautiful creation of pookalam, where everyone contributes colorful flowers to craft intricate designs at the school entrance. It‚Äôs a wonderful opportunity for collaboration and creativity! As the festivities continue, we come together for a special Onam sadhya, featuring a delightful array of traditional dishes prepared with love and care. Sharing this meal fosters a sense of community, as we sit together and enjoy the delicious flavors of Kerala. The celebrations also include traditional games and cultural performances, showcasing the talents of both teachers and staff.
